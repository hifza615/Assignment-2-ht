New Science Lab Opens at Federal High School

The Federal High School proudly inaugurated its new science laboratory today. Students and teachers were excited to see the upgraded equipment and bright new workspace designed to encourage experiments and discoveries.

The principal announced that this step aims to improve practical learning for biology, chemistry, and physics students. Regular workshops and science fairs will also be conducted throughout the year to enhance creativity and innovation.
